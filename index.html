About Chemical Elements

The periodic table is a list of all known chemical elements, arranged by atomic number and electron configuration. Each element is unique, with its own properties and uses. Some elements are essential for life, while others are used in technology, industry, and many other applications.

For example, Hydrogen (H) is the lightest element and is a key building block for all matter in the universe. It is also crucial for the energy production process in stars, including the Sun.

On the other hand, elements like Iron (Fe) are critical for life as they are a key component of hemoglobin in our blood, which is responsible for carrying oxygen throughout our bodies.
